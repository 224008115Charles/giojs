Instruction

GIO Playground is a fast and convenient tool for you to customize your own Gio globe. There are three different aspects of settings that you can control, you can follow the instructions listed below.

Control

  • Show Stats: Show a stats panel on the top left corner
  • Disable Unmentioned: Make unmentioned countries not clickable
  • Lighten Mentioned: Lighten the mentioned countries
  • In Only: Only show lines toward selected country
  • Out Only: Only show lines go out of selected country
  • Halo: Show halo

Color

  • Surface: Pick a color for globe surface (without selected country)
  • Selected Country: Pick a color for selected country
  • In Line: Pick a color for lines toward selected country
  • Out Line: Pick a color for lines go out of selected country
  • Halo: Pick a color for halo surrounding globe
  • Background: Pick a color for background

Brightness

  • Ocean: Adjust brightness for ocean area
  • Mentioned: Adjust brightness for mentioned countries
  • Related: Adjust brightness for related countries
